The Story

Shell Design Elements was started by artist Kristin Carde in Brandon, Florida. Her design inspirations come from the love of the beach and the love of shells which was formed from a childhood growing up near the Gulf Coast of Florida near Anna Maria Island. Later extensive travels to exotic beaches around the world enabled her to fashion each of her creations from personal experiences of blissful beach moments. While also an avid painter and muralist, her passion is demonstrated in her unique and elegant shell creations.

A Note From The Artist: Shells have been adored for centuries and have been used as currency, tools, religious and spiritual objects, musical instruments, personal adornment, architectural decoration, and of course... ART.

The beautiful seashell has it's place in home décor as well. In the Chinese art of creating a harmonious environment, the idea is that the space we live in has an effect on us; emotionally, physically, even spiritually. Shells symbolize what we desire like happy relationships, prosperity, and good health. We all wish to attract that energy into our lives. Shells represent a comfortable home and a feeling of contentment. They often evoke a memory of childhood days at the beach and also represent the beautiful tropical paradise of our dreams where there is no stress or worries, and lots of sunshine. Bring the sunshine and happiness of shells into your décor.

Construction Details

All hand constructed wood frames are built with screws and glue and secured with metal corner brackets for extra strength. Hardware is bolted directly through entire frame for additional support along with heavy duty hanging wire attached to durable D ring metal hangers.

All hanging hardware comes with mirror which is ready to hang when delivered.

Mirrors are custom made for each order out of high quality ¼" beveled mirror.
